21 Feb 2023

Aptitude Test by RAESA

On 21st February 2023, RAESA BVDUCOE, Pune conducted an aptitude test for the Department of Robotics and Automation. The test consisted of logical, quantitative, verbal, and non-verbal tests. The test was mandatory for all 1st, 2nd, and 3rd-year students of the department.
The test was conducted from 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM at the Robotics and Automation Department. The venue was well-organized and equipped with all the necessary arrangements. The students arrived on time and were provided with the required materials to take the test.
The logical and quantitative test comprised of questions that tested the student’s reasoning and mathematical abilities. The verbal test assessed the student’s language and comprehension skills, whereas the non-verbal test evaluated the student’s ability to analyze patterns and shapes.
The students were well-prepared and showed great enthusiasm while taking the test. They appeared to be confident and determined to showcase their abilities. The test was challenging, but the students were able to complete it on time.
The event was a great success, and the students appreciated the efforts made by the RAESA team in organizing the test. The event was beneficial in identifying the strengths and weaknesses of the students and helped them in improving their aptitude skills.
In conclusion, the aptitude test conducted by RAESA BVDUCOE was an excellent initiative, and we hope to see more such events in the future. We congratulate the team on their success and wish them all the best for their future endeavors.
