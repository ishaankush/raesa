The Robotics and Automation Engineering Students Association (RAESA) organized a Chess Competition on 20th February 2023. The event aimed to provide a platform for students to showcase their chess skills and win exciting prizes.
The competition was held in the Robotics and Automation Department and commenced at 1 PM. It consisted of a series of chess matches where the winner advanced to the next level until we crowned the Chess Champion for the year 2023.
The event was open to all students. The response was overwhelming, with students from various departments of the college participating.
