11 Nov 2023

Seminar on Raspberry Pi Microcontroller

RAESA organized a seminar titled “Raspberry Pi Review: Ultimate Beginner's Guide” on 11th Nov. The venue for this event was WS408. The Objective of this seminar was to provide a brief introduction to the power and capabilities of a development board like Raspberry Pi and its extensive practical applicability.
The guest speakers for this event were Mr. Shrikar Nakhye and Mr. Akshay Kelkar from Hunro Pro Robotics.
Prof. Lembhe and Prof. Jadhav facilitated the guest speakers at the beginning of the event. The seminar was started by Mr. Akshay with a couple of activities with the attendants. Then Mr. Shrikar took over and started with basic concepts like differences between Microcontroller and Microprocessor. Proceeded to brief explanation and differences of various development boards like Arduino, Raspberry Pi, Nvidia Jetson. He then explained the specifications of Arduino Uno board along with comparison to specifications of Raspberry Pi. Followed by an example of interfacing of a servo motor with the Arduino Board.
The Seminar then moved on to Raspberry Pi, the speaker then informed the attendants with Specifications of Raspberry Pi development board like WiFi Compatibility of 2.4GHz as well as 5GHz frequency which is the latest development in WiFi Technology.
The CPU of Raspberry Pi has a clock frequency of 1.5GHz, which is a 4 Core CPU, and also has a 1 Core GPU. Presence of GPU adds the ability of Camera integration with Raspberry Pi. The speaker then practically interfaced a camera with a Raspberry Pi board for demonstration.
Followed by a Question-Answer Session.
The Vice President of RAESA Khushi Patel concluded the event with a vote of thanks
